Why Families Choose The Academy

A preschool experience shaped by care, curiosity, and Christian values.

The Academy at Versailles UMC is more than just a preschool. It is a place where faith and learning meet, where children are encouraged to explore, and where families are supported along the way. ([academy.versaillesumc.org](https://academy.versaillesumc.org/home))

Confident and Curious Learners

Children are encouraged through hands-on experiences, creativity, discovery, and play-filled learning. ([academy.versaillesumc.org](https://academy.versaillesumc.org/home))

Caring, Qualified Teachers

The Academy emphasizes caring staff, engaging curriculum, and support for each child’s academic, social, emotional, and spiritual growth. ([academy.versaillesumc.org](https://academy.versaillesumc.org/home))

Christian Values

Daily life at The Academy weaves in biblical truth and reminds children that they are wonderfully made and deeply loved by God. ([academy.versaillesumc.org](https://academy.versaillesumc.org/home))

Support for Families

The Academy presents itself as a community of encouragement and care that extends beyond the classroom. ([academy.versaillesumc.org](https://academy.versaillesumc.org/home))

About The Academy

A warm and welcoming place for children and families.

The Academy describes its mission as partnering with families to raise children who are confident, curious, and grounded in God’s truth. The goal is to nurture children academically, socially, emotionally, and spiritually in a setting where they feel safe, supported, and loved. ([academy.versaillesumc.org](https://academy.versaillesumc.org/home))

Enrollment Information

Helpful details for parents.

The current Academy site lists application and enrollment steps, food details, parent handbook information, and outdoor playground improvements completed in 2025. ([academy.versaillesumc.org](https://academy.versaillesumc.org/home))

Enrollment Steps

  • Application, enrollment interview, and deposit required. ([academy.versaillesumc.org](https://academy.versaillesumc.org/home))
  • Parents receive a copy of the Parent Handbook. ([academy.versaillesumc.org](https://academy.versaillesumc.org/home))

Meals and Daily Care

  • Breakfast and snacks are included in tuition. ([academy.versaillesumc.org](https://academy.versaillesumc.org/home))
  • Parents provide a packed lunch each day. ([academy.versaillesumc.org](https://academy.versaillesumc.org/home))

Quality and Standards

  • Listed as a 2 rating in Kentucky All STARS. ([academy.versaillesumc.org](https://academy.versaillesumc.org/home))
  • State-approved artificial ground covering added to the outdoor playground in 2025. ([academy.versaillesumc.org](https://academy.versaillesumc.org/home))
