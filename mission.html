The Academy Mission Statement

At The Academy, our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing, and Christ-centered environment where children can grow academically, socially, and spiritually. We believe every child is created in the image of God, uniquely gifted, and worthy of love, care, and guidance.

Grounded in the vision of Versailles United Methodist Church—to reach toward God and one another, grow people into passionate followers of Jesus, and send people out to bring others to Christ—our Academy seeks to embody this calling in the lives of young children and their families.

We strive to:

  • Reach children and families by offering compassionate care, encouragement, and opportunities to experience God’s love in everyday learning and play.
  • Grow children in a developmentally appropriate, faith-based environment that nurtures curiosity, creativity, and character, while planting seeds of faith that will last a lifetime.
  • Send children and families into the world equipped with a strong foundation of Christian values, love for others, and confidence in who God created them to be.

Through intentional teaching, loving guidance, and a partnership with families, The Academy provides more than childcare or education—we provide a safe Christian community where children are inspired to thrive, and where faith and learning grow hand in hand.
