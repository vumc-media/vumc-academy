About Me

My name is Brittany Sholar. I just started working here at The Academy. I have been working with children for about 15 years in many different atmospheres from Montessori to Reggio and traditional ways of teaching. I love to mix it all together and incorporate them all in a classroom. I have been married to my husband Adam for almost 14 years and we have three children Brooklynn (13), Greyson (9), and Courtney (7). We have a few pets, a cat named Merlin, and dogs named Lucy, Deacon, and Mya. As you can tell, we love animals. I love watching children learn and grow in all aspects of life. I'm so excited to meet you and your little ones and welcome them to a year of learning new things!
