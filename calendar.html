Quick Answers

What first-time guests usually want to know.

We want your first Sunday to feel simple, not stressful. Here are a few helpful things to know before you arrive.

What should I wear?

Come as you are. Some dress casually, some dress up a little more, and you are welcome either way.

Is there something for children?

Yes. We offer children’s ministry and family-friendly spaces to help your Sunday morning go smoothly.

Where do I park?

Parking is available on site, and we want to make your arrival as easy as possible from the moment you pull in.

Can I watch online first?

Absolutely. You can get a feel for worship and the church atmosphere before visiting in person.

Sunday Mornings

A welcoming place to worship, learn, and connect.

Sunday mornings at VUMC are designed to help people of all ages encounter God, grow in faith, and feel part of a caring community. Whether you are attending alone, with a spouse, with children, or with friends, we want you to feel at home.

What To Expect

A few things that can make your first visit easier.

Here is a quick overview of what you can expect when you come to Versailles United Methodist Church.

Two worship styles

Our 9:30 AM traditional service offers a more classic worship atmosphere, while our 10:30 AM contemporary service has a more modern feel.

Children and families

Families matter here. We want children to feel welcomed and parents to feel supported as they worship and connect.

Location and arrival

You can find us at 230 Paynes Mill Road, Versailles, Kentucky 40383. Parking is available on site for guests and regular attenders alike.

No pressure

You do not need to know everything before coming. Just show up, breathe, and let us help you feel at ease.

Let Us Know You’re Coming

We would love to welcome you well.

Fill out the visit form so we can help make your first Sunday feel easier. Whether you want directions, have questions, or just want someone to expect you, we are glad to help.
